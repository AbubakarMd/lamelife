Harnaaz sandhu became 3rd indian to win the tittle of Miss Universe!

14th december, 2021

21 years old Harnaaz Sandhu wonn the tittle MISS UNIVERSE 2021 bringing the 3rd tittle for india after 21 years. Sushmita sen and lara dutta have won this tittle before in 1994 and 2000 respectively.



The new Miss Universe is...India!!!! #MISSUNIVERSE pic.twitter.com/DTiOKzTHl4

— Miss Universe (@MissUniverse) December 13, 2021

Harnaaz sandhu have represented india in 70th Miss universe held in Eilat, Israel. She have defeated Paraguay's Nadia Ferreira and South Africa's Lalela Mswane.

On asking WHAT ADVICE Would you give to young women watching on how to deal with the pressure they face today, This is what she have to say!

FINAL STATEMENT: India. #MISSUNIVERSE

The 70th MISS UNIVERSE Competition is airing LIVE around the world from Eilat, Israel on @foxtv pic.twitter.com/wwyMhsAyvd

— Miss Universe (@MissUniverse) December 13, 2021

Harnaaz Sandhu is chandigarh based Model who have previously won many tittles such as MISS DIVA 2021, FEMINA MISS PUNJAB 2019 and many more

About her family, Her mother is Senior Mohana Officer at Sohana Hospital and her father is in Real State Business.
