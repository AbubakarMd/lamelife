BGIS 2021 INFORMATION

1st december, 2021



Ragistration for Battleground mobile india finally came to an end! player from all over India have ragistered their team.
This is the first open event for every BGMI players with prizepool of 1 crore. winner to be awarded with 50 lakhs.

Ingame qualifier will begin from 2nd december,2021 for all teams. Online qualifier will begin from 16th december between 1024 qualified team in 3 rounds and will end by 30th december. some invited will play practice scrims from 4th december. We will tell you the list of all invited players.



BGIS Schedule:

ingame Qualifier : 4th december to 8th december. (1024 teams will qualify)
Online qualifier : 16th december to 30th december. (64 teams will qualify)
Quater final : 2nd jan to 5th jan (24 teams will qualify)
semi final : 7th jan to 10th jan (16 teams qualify)
Grand final: 13th jan to 16th jan (Will be invited to PMGC)

The team winning BGIS might get invitation in Global Championship.


One the other side, some teams have been invited to practice BGMI scrims, which will start from 4th december.

List of invited teams:
group A: Team xo,orgless 5, Hydra, Velocity gaming, AXOM Esports, Team Insane, Chemin Esports, Team forever

Group b: Team soul, Global esports, Hydrabad Hydras, Marcos gaming, Orangutan, Blind Esports, True rippers, Nigma Galaxy

Group c: Godlike Esports, 7seas esports, Revenant esports, Skylight gaming, 8bit, Enigma gaming, Rivalry Esports, Team mayhem

Group d: Team Xspark, Burnx official, or esports, rackoning esports, team incognito, force one, udog indiia, TSM
